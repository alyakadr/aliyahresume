CORE COMPETENCIES

These foundational skills have laid the groundwork for my future career in the technology industry, allowing me to excel in managing computer systems and leading projects effectively.

I'm also very interested in the latest technology trends outside of the university. As a tech enthusiast, I always seek ways to learn more and improve my skills. Because I am so interested in technology, I can adapt to the changing nature of the industry and welcome new developments.
